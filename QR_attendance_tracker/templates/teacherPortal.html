Welcome To Teacher's Portal
Attendance made easy with our new QR generated process

Guidelines for Teacher:

  • Inform students about the QR based attendance system and the specific procedure they need to follow for attendance marking.
  • Ensure that each student has a unique QR code for attendance marking.
  • Train the students on how to generate their QR codes for attendance marking and how to display it for scanning.
  • Ensure that the QR scanner is working properly before starting the attendance marking process.
  • Make sure that the QR scanner is capable of scanning all the students' QR codes, and there is no delay in the scanning process.
  • Instruct students to display their QR codes in a visible and accessible position for easy scanning.
  • Check the attendance list after every scanning to ensure the accuracy of the recorded attendance.
  • Make sure to record attendance only for the students who are present in the classroom during the time of attendance marking.
  • Instruct students to wait until their QR codes are successfully scanned before leaving the classroom.
  • In case of any technical issues, keep a backup plan ready for manual attendance marking.
